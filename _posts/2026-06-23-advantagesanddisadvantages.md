# According to some people, it would be better if industries are established in regions outside the urban area. Discuss its advantages and disadvantages.

## Write an essay of about 250 words on the following topics.
Few individuals believe that it is more fruitful if companies are set up in areas outside the densely populated regions. This essay will discuss the pros and cons of this notion. 

To begin with, the pollution levels in the cities will reduce drastically when all the industries are established miles away from their boundaries. This will decrease the cases related to air and water borne diseases such as Jaundice, Lung Cancer, and other respiratory problems. In addition to this, residents will face less traffic in their locality. This will assist them in reaching their friends house in short time. For instance, a bunch of families living in urban areas reported that they inhale pure air because of lower AQI in their city as industries are not situated near them.

On the other hand, there are only few opportunities in inner-city  if there is no firm near the border of a metropolitan. Most of the young people are not able to find jobs in nearby districts so they leave their residence to grow in their career. As a result, these places are full of older people who need care takers to help them in their daily tasks. Unfortunately, senior citizens are struggling in getting homecare personals  in their community. For example, a group of ten employers in this area cannot hire the public support workers  because of their shortage.

To sum up, common citizens will breath fresh air and their travel time will be reduced if the manufacturing units are away from them but this can create joblessness  and force youth to leave their home.
