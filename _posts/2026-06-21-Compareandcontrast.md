# Some people think that learning online is more efficient. However, according to some other people, studying from books is still the preferred method. Which is the better method of learning out of the two?

## Write an essay of about 250 words on the following topics.

A few individuals believe that eLearning  is more productive. In contrast, some people think that it is better to use handouts  for studying. This essay will discuss the pros of virtual learning and the cons of book learning.

To begin with, students can learn online from teachers who are not available in their country . This can help them in learning different subjects such as Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from the best professors in the word. In addition to this, they can schedule their classes as per their convenience. For instance, my friend is learning coding in python from a foreign tutor while sitting at home after completing his day shift.

On the other hand, ordinary people who want to study cannot buy the hard copy of notes because it is very expensive for them. If someone donates study material to these poor student, then it becomes difficult to study from these old decaying books. Another problem which is faced by majority of the students is they are not able to understand concepts by reading the chapters. Many authors assume that the readers can understand the context without giving  proper explanation .For example, in Grade 10, I tried to study Algebra by myself but was not able to understand the concept of Line.

To sum up, there are many benefits of digital learning and disadvantages of using printed notes in this modern world so it is better  to study online. Because, it is more convenient and you can hire a teacher from any part of the world. 

# A growing number of people own personal vehicle as they find it a convenient mode of transport in comparison to public transport. Compare the public and private mode of transportation and state which is better out of the two.

## Write an essay of about 250 words on the following topics.

Nowadays, many people are buying private automobiles  because they believe that it is better than public transport. This essay will discuss the benefits of using personal vehicles and the problems in the  transit system.

To begin with, commuters can reduce their travel time by using their own cars, scooters, trucks, and bikes which can save money in fuel and reduce the carbon emissions. Also, they can do private conversation with their friends and family members inside their own car. In addition to this, travellers can go to any destination without worrying about the time of the day. For instance, senior citizens who are having medical issues can visit the nearby hospital even at night.

On the other hand, government  buses are not safe for the ordinary citizens. Many people are scared in travelling via daily transit because of pick pocketing, chain snatching and bullying. Also, they are having dirty seats and extremely poor  ventilation system. If a person tries to go to his office using these four wheelers  then there is a good chance that he will be late because most of these buses  are old, slow and are not on time. For example, in Grade 10, I used to go to my school via Delhi Transportation Corporation  service in which you need to protect yourself from thieves. 

To sum up, majority of the commuters prefer using their own vehicle because it is more safe and reliable as compared to public transport in which anyone can become victim of a crime.
