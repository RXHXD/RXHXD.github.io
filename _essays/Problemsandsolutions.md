# Internet has completely transformed the way information is shared but this has led to many serious problems. What are the problems being faced due to this? Suggest some possible solutions for dealing with these problems.

## Write an essay of about 250 words on the following topics.

Internet has completely changed the mechanism by which information is shared but this has led to many dangerous problems. This essay will discuss about some problems which are faced due to internet with their solutions.

There are many problems which are faced by people because of the internet. Firstly, some websites sell their personal data to the marketing companies without taking their permissions. Secondly, hackers can steal their personal photos and videos from social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat to extort money from them. Finally, someone  can use your identity to do cyber fraud with your friends and family members. For example, a person tried to do rent a car by using my electronic identity card.

The government can run online campaign to spread awareness about cyber frauds and what steps you can take to prevent it.

To sum up, problems such as data leak, extortion, and digital fraud are caused because of the internet and to solve these problems we should use a strong password, the government officials should run advertisement to spread awareness, and the people should clear the cookies of the websites which they are browsing on the internet.
